# Privacy Policy

**Effective date:** July 2026

AQW Wiki Enhanced is designed with privacy as a core principle. The extension performs its work locally inside your browser and does not require you to create an account, enter your password, or send your inventory to any third-party service.

## What information the extension accesses

The extension accesses only the information necessary to provide its features.

### AdventureQuest Worlds Wiki

While you browse `https://aqwwiki.wikidot.com`, the extension reads page content to:

- mark items you own
- calculate merge recipes and quest requirements
- display hover previews
- provide search enhancements
- improve the wiki interface

This processing happens entirely inside your browser.

### Your AQW account

The extension reads your inventory and bank information from your existing authenticated session at `https://account.aq.com`.

The extension **does not**:

- ask for your username or password
- provide its own login form
- store your login credentials
- transmit your inventory to any third party

Your browser's existing AQW session is used exactly as if you visited the Manage Account website yourself.

### Public Artix calendar

The extension retrieves publicly available server boost information from Artix in order to display current and upcoming server boosts.

## Information stored locally

The extension stores information on your computer using your browser's extension storage, including:

- cached inventory data
- Farm Goals
- theme preference
- search preferences
- extension settings
- telemetry consent preference

This information remains on your device unless you remove the extension or clear its data.

## Optional anonymous telemetry

Telemetry is **disabled by default**.

If you explicitly choose to enable anonymous telemetry, the extension sends one anonymous report approximately once per week containing only aggregate feature usage counts, the extension version, the browser family (Chromium or Firefox) and the active theme (Good or Evil).

Examples include:

- popup openings
- searches performed
- Armory openings
- hover previews shown
- successful or failed inventory synchronizations

The telemetry **never includes**:

- passwords
- account credentials
- usernames
- character names
- inventory contents
- item names
- URLs you visited
- wiki pages
- cookies
- IP-based user identification
- persistent identifiers

Telemetry is used only to understand which features are being used and to help identify bugs or compatibility problems.

You can disable telemetry at any time.

## Data sharing

The extension communicates only with:

- `aqwwiki.wikidot.com`
- `account.aq.com`
- `www.artix.com`
- the optional telemetry endpoint (only if you have opted in)

No personal information is sold, rented, or shared with advertisers or data brokers.

## Data retention

If telemetry is enabled, anonymous aggregate usage reports are retained for 90 days to produce usage statistics. These reports contain no personal information and cannot be used to identify individual users.

## Open source

AQW Wiki Enhanced is free and open source software licensed under GPL-3.0.

The source code is publicly available, including the telemetry implementation, allowing anyone to inspect exactly what the extension does.

## Changes to this policy

If the extension's privacy practices change, this Privacy Policy will be updated accordingly.
